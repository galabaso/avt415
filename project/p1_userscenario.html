[project #1: user scenario]

scenario

june is a freshman in college looking to get organized and stay on top of things as she also works at the local pastry shop during the weekends. she always forgets when her papers and projects are due, as well as chores or errands she needs to get done. june finds april house, an online stationery store, and decides to get a journal/planner for this upcoming semester. she's also interested in getting more pens and stickers to make journaling a bit more fun.

actor

june the student

motivator

forgets due dates and personal errands

intention

for maximum productivity, to get organized, and to stay on top of things

action

june looks through an online stationery store and gets herself a planner

resolution

she buys a planner along with a set of colored pens and stickers to make planning a bit more fun to do
